# The National Heart Clinic — Branding Notes

## Colors
- **Primary Dark Navy**: #1B2A4A (dark navy blue header/nav bar)
- **Header bg**: Very dark navy/charcoal ~#1e293b or darker
- **Text on dark**: White
- **Body text**: Dark charcoal/near-black
- **Accent/CTA buttons**: Green (#2d8a4e or similar sage/forest green) — "Our Specialists" button
- **Secondary button**: White/outlined
- **Background**: Clean white/very light
- **Section headings**: Dark navy
- **Insurance logos bar**: Light gray background

## Typography
- **Headings**: Serif font — appears to be a classic serif (similar to Playfair Display or similar)
- **"THE NATIONAL HEART CLINIC"** logo text: All caps, spaced, serif
- **Subtext**: "COMPLETE CARDIAC CARE" — smaller, spaced
- **Body**: Clean sans-serif

## Visual Style
- Professional, clinical, trustworthy
- Clean white backgrounds
- Dark navy header bar
- Minimal use of color — navy + green accents
- Photography: Building exterior, professional
- Insurance partner logos displayed prominently
- Overall feel: Premium medical, Harley Street quality

## Key Brand Elements
- Dark navy top bar
- Green CTA buttons
- Serif headings
- Clean, spacious layout
- Professional and understated
